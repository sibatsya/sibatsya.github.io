Frequently Asked Questions
I have never coded before. Can I still do this?+
Yes, absolutely. Module 1 assumes zero programming knowledge. Students with no coding background have successfully completed this course.
How long will it take to complete?+
Approximately 45 hours of video content. At 1 hour per day, you can complete it in 6-8 weeks. You have lifetime access - go at your own pace.

This is a Core ABAP course. All concepts apply to both SAP ECC and S/4HANA. S/4HANA-specific topics like CDS Views and AMDP are introduced in Module 9.

Every lecture has a Q&A section. Post your question and the instructor will respond. You are never learning alone.
